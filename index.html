About US

Hi, We are here to take you through the history again but this time from a feminine lens!

History often overlooks the contributions of women. Matri sheds light on these inspiring stories, ensuring their rightful place in history. By recognizing their achievements, we inspire future generations to fight for their dreams, regardless of gender.
Matri is an innovative VR museum dedicated to these remarkable women. Using cutting-edge VR technology, we bring their stories to life in an immersive and interactive experience.
